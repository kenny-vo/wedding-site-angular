FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I arrive for the ceremony?

Please arrive no later than 5:45pm as the ceremony will start promptly at 6pm.

There will be traffic on Friday afternoon, so check Google Maps on event day to make sure you can get to the venue on time.

Is there a dress code?

Yes, formal wear.

Is parking available?

Plenty! The venue has a large parking lot (free) and drop off area if you take an Uber/Lyft.

Anything else we should know about the venue?

The ceremony and reception will be outdoors, so there is some uneven flooring. Also, bring a light jacket or wrap for the evening.

Can I bring my children?

Only children in the wedding party and immediate family will be in attendance.

What if I have dietary restrictions/allergies?

Please let us know in the notes section when you RSVP.

What time does the reception end?

The DJ will play the last song at 10:55pm.

Gifts/Registry?

We don’t have a registry. You being here on our special day is the best gift we could ask for. If you’d still like to get us something, cash gifts are customary in the Chinese/Vietnamese culture.
